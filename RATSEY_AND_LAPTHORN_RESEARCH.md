# Ratsey & Lapthorn — site research

**Source:** Public pages on [ratseyandlapthorn.com](https://ratseyandlapthorn.com/) (crawled for reference).  
**Context:** One of Simon’s businesses (per project note). This document summarises positioning, offers, locations, and commercial mechanics as stated on the site — not independent verification.

---

## Executive summary

**Ratsey & Lapthorn** presents as the **world’s oldest sailmaker** (founded **1790**), combining heritage hand craft with modern design and materials. Core revenue streams visible on the site: **custom sails** (classic, cruising, racing), **covers and awnings** (marine, residential, military, industrial, aerospace), **service & repair**, and a **limited handmade bag collection (2025)** with **NFC traceability**. Two production **lofts**: **Cowes, Isle of Wight (UK)** and **Barcelona (Spain)**.

**Brand line:** *“Experts in sailmaking for over 235 years.”*  
**Heritage quote (repeated):** *“There is only one standard of work in this loft, that is the very best.”* — Tom W. Ratsey, **1833**.

## Product lines (sailmaking)

From [Sailmaking](https://ratseyandlapthorn.com/sailmaking):

1. **Classic** — Aesthetic aligned with original yacht design; modern performance; quote from Tom W. Ratsey 1833 featured prominently.
2. **Cruising** — Durability and manageability; coastal/offshore/extreme latitudes; Dacron, laminates, high-tenacity fibres as needed.
3. **Racing** — Classic and contemporary; CAD/laser-cut; laminates, wovens, carbon from partners; America’s Cup, J Class, etc.
4. **Covers and awnings** — Boom covers, awnings, full boat covers, hardware/thatch covers; **private residences**; **military, industrial, aerospace**; bespoke projects.

**Related capabilities** (FAQ): Film/TV supply; unusual bespoke enquiries encouraged. **Masts and rigging:** not supplied; work with naval architects and riggers.

**Enquiries:** [Request a quote](mailto:info@ratseyandlapthorn.com?subject=Enquiry) (as linked from sailmaking page).
