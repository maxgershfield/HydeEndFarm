## The Vineyard

Hyde End is a new English vineyard established with a clear and deliberate philosophy:

**To produce exceptional English wine with a disciplined focus on quality over quantity.**

The vineyard comprises **15,000 vines across 7 acres**.

We expect to produce approximately **15,000 bottles per year on average** — roughly one bottle per vine. This is below typical industry yields and reflects a conscious decision:

**Quality over quantity**

Production has already begun, and the vineyard is now entering its formative years, with character and consistency developing vintage by vintage.
